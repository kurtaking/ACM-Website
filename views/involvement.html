HackRice
Jan. 15-17, 2016

Two of our members traveled to Rice University in Houston to compete in a hackathon against schools from all over the nation. For the 36-hour competition, the UTSA team developed a multi-player investment game in which the stock prices of companies are controlled by the positive and negative tweets. For each positive tweet about the company the stock price rises, and for each negative tweet, it falls. Their hard work paid off, winning them the top spot in the PROS competition.
