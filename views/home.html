Who We Are?

We are The University of Texas at San Antonio student chapter of ACM. The Association for Computing Machinery is the largest international society for educational and scientific computing. What we are not, though, is just your ordinary computer science club, instead we invite any student that has a passion for technology to join our growing community. Our aim is to build a network not only between our members, but to also incorporate our professional connections. This interaction helps us achieve one of our goals by creating opportunities to prepare our members for the prosperous careers we know are ahead of them.
